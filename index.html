About Me

I'm a dedicated professional with a degree in Informatics Engineering. My passion lies in crafting innovative digital solutions, and I bring a wealth of experience across multiple domains of technology.

My background spans web development, email development, database development, and domain & hosting setup. I thrive on turning complex challenges into seamless, user-friendly experiences.

I also work as an email developer, creating responsive and customized templates for email campaigns. I’ve built effective solutions for various platforms, including Klaviyo, Braze, and Iterable, ensuring optimal delivery and engagement.

I’m proficient in a variety of programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Liquid, PHP, Blade, and SQL. On the frontend, I leverage frameworks and libraries like jQuery, Bootstrap, and Materialize to create responsive and dynamic interfaces.

In the backend realm, I work with PHP, Laravel, and SQL to build robust and scalable systems. Additionally, I have extensive experience with CMS platforms such as WordPress and Shopify, enabling me to deliver tailored solutions for diverse needs.

As a plus, I handle design tools like Photoshop and Figma with a solid understanding. These skills enhance my ability to create visually appealing and well-structured designs that complement my development work.

Beyond technical skills, I’m multilingual and fluent in Spanish, English, and French. This allows me to collaborate effectively with international teams and clients, bringing a global perspective to my work.
